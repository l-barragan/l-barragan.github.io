Brick Pavilion

Barragan’s Pavilion takes the form of an enclosed space for a self-introspection, based in Mexico’s architecture and brick materials. The installation is also a reference to the Greenwich Meridian, the global standard marker of time and geographical distance, presenting playful and original perspectives that invites the visitors to interact with time and space, sun, shadows and geometry.
